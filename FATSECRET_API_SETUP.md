## 🚀 Quick Setup (5 Minutes)

### Step 1: Create Account

Go to: **https://platform.fatsecret.com/api/**

1. Click "Get Started" or "Sign Up"
2. Fill in your details:
   - Email
   - Password
   - Company/Project name: "Daily Discipline Trainer"
3. Verify your email

### Step 2: Apply for Premier Free Tier

1. After logging in, go to: **https://platform.fatsecret.com/upgrade-account**
2. Select **"Premier Free"** plan
3. Fill in the application:
   - **Project Type**: Startup / Student / Non-profit
   - **Description**: "Fitness tracking mobile app for personal health"
   - **Attribution**: "Yes, I will add attribution"
4. Submit application
5. Usually approved within 24 hours (often instant!)

### Step 3: Create API Credentials

1. Go to your dashboard
2. Click "Create Application" or "My Applications"
3. Fill in:
   - **Application Name**: "Daily Discipline Trainer"
   - **Description**: "Fitness and nutrition tracking app"
4. Click "Create"
5. You'll see TWO keys:
   - **Consumer Key** (like: `abc123def456`)
   - **Consumer Secret** (like: `xyz789uvw012`)
6. Copy BOTH keys

### Step 4: Send Me Your Keys

Send me both keys in this format:
```
FatSecret Consumer Key: abc123def456
FatSecret Consumer Secret: xyz789uvw012
```

I'll add them to your app immediately!
