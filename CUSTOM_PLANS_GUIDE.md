# 📅 Custom Plans Feature Guide

## Overview
The Daily Discipline Trainer now supports fully customizable workout and diet plans! Users can create their own exercise routines, meal plans, and weekly schedules, or continue using the auto-generated plans.

## Features Added

### 1. Custom Workout Plans 💪
- Create personalized workout routines with custom exercises
- Define sets, reps, rest times, and calories per set
- Organize exercises by muscle groups
- Reorder exercises with drag-and-drop
- Track total calories burned per workout

### 2. Custom Diet Plans 🍽️
- Design personalized meal plans with custom meals
- Set meal times and food descriptions
- Define calories and macros (protein, carbs, fat) for each meal
- Set daily calorie goals
- Reorder meals throughout the day

### 3. Weekly Schedules 📅
- Create complete weekly training schedules
- Mix and match:
  - Auto-generated workout categories (HIIT, Strength, etc.)
  - Your custom workout plans
  - Rest days
- Assign diet plans (auto-generated or custom)
- Switch between schedules anytime

## How to Use

### Accessing Custom Plans
1. Open the app and go to **Settings** (bottom navigation)
2. Tap on **"MY CUSTOM PLANS"** at the top
3. You'll see three tabs:
   - **SCHEDULES**: Manage weekly training schedules
   - **WORKOUTS**: Create custom workout routines
   - **DIET**: Design custom meal plans

### Creating a Custom Workout
1. Go to **Settings → My Custom Plans → WORKOUTS**
2. Tap **"+ CREATE NEW WORKOUT"**
3. Enter workout name and description
4. Tap **"+ ADD EXERCISE"** to add exercises:
   - Exercise name (e.g., "Bench Press")
   - Muscle group (Chest, Back, Shoulders, etc.)
   - Sets, reps, rest time
   - Calories per set
   - Optional notes
5. Reorder exercises by dragging
6. Tap **"SAVE WORKOUT"**

### Creating a Custom Diet Plan
1. Go to **Settings → My Custom Plans → DIET**
2. Tap **"+ CREATE NEW DIET PLAN"**
3. Enter plan name, description, and daily calorie goal
4. Tap **"+ ADD MEAL"** to add meals:
   - Meal name and time (e.g., "Breakfast", "8:00 AM")
   - Foods description
   - Calories and macros (protein, carbs, fat)
5. Reorder meals by dragging
6. Tap **"SAVE DIET PLAN"**

### Creating a Weekly Schedule
1. Go to **Settings → My Custom Plans → SCHEDULES**
2. Tap **"+ CREATE NEW SCHEDULE"**
3. Enter schedule name
4. For each day of the week:
   - Tap to assign a workout (auto-generated category, custom workout, or rest day)
   - Leave blank for flexible days
5. Select a diet plan (auto-generated or custom)
6. Tap **"SAVE SCHEDULE"**

### Activating a Schedule
1. Go to **Settings → My Custom Plans → SCHEDULES**
2. Tap on any schedule to activate it
3. The selected schedule will show a checkmark
4. Your home screen will now follow this schedule
5. To return to auto-generated plans, select **"Auto-Generated Plan"**

## Key Benefits

### Flexibility
- **Two Options**: Use auto-generated plans OR create your own
- **Mix & Match**: Combine auto-generated workouts with custom ones
- **Easy Switching**: Change schedules anytime without losing data

### Personalization
- **Your Goals**: Design plans that match your specific fitness goals
- **Your Schedule**: Set workout and meal times that fit your lifestyle
- **Your Preferences**: Choose exercises and foods you actually enjoy

### Progress Tracking
- All custom workouts integrate with the existing progress tracking
- Points, streaks, and badges work the same way
- Custom exercises count toward your daily goals

## Tips

1. **Start Simple**: Create one custom workout or meal plan first to get familiar
2. **Use Templates**: Base your custom plans on the auto-generated ones
3. **Be Realistic**: Set achievable goals for sets, reps, and calories
4. **Experiment**: Try different schedules to find what works best for you
5. **Track Progress**: Monitor how your custom plans affect your results

## Data Persistence
- All custom plans are saved locally on your device
- Plans persist across app restarts
- You can create unlimited workouts, diet plans, and schedules
- Delete plans you no longer need to keep things organized
